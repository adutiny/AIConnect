  • What is AI really?

    Artificial Intelligence, or AI, refers to the ability of machines or computer programs to perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence to complete. In the context of conversation, AI refers to the use of computer programs or bots that can communicate with humans in a natural language, similar to how humans would communicate with each other. These bots use a combination of natural language processing, machine learning, and other advanced techniques to understand the meaning of the words spoken or typed by humans and generate appropriate responses. The goal of conversational AI is to create a more natural and intuitive way for humans to interact with computers and technology, making it easier to perform tasks and access information.


  • What does it mean to train AI?

    Training AI is like teaching a computer program to learn things, like how you learn in school. You show the program many examples(Datasets), and it learns from them to recognize patterns and make predictions as to the answer. The more examples(datasets) it sees, the better it gets at making accurate answers. The goal is to create a computer program that can answer questions like a smart student in school. A simple example would be a set of questions with the corrosponding answers would be uploaded to the AI, now it has more specific information about your comapny and or specific clients to generate a very accurate answer.
